Introduction

Mokete Elias Tyabekana is a versatile IT professional with over 4 years of experience in information technology, software development, and graphic design. Currently working as an Information Technology Officer at Botshabelo Digital Hub, he provides IT support, manages software and hardware maintenance, and mentors software development interns. Mokete is passionate about helping start-up entrepreneurs in the IT and robotics sectors turn their ideas into commercial products, while also managing Google Admin and office printer systems.

With a diploma in Information Technology from Central University of Technology and certifications in web design and network administration, Mokete is skilled in front-end development, desktop application development, and IT support. His dedication to continuous learning is demonstrated through his recent ALX Professional Foundations certification and his work as a part-time graphic designer for Digital Market Graphics.
